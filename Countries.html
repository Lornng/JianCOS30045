Top 10 Asian Countries with the highest immigration (Origin)

Immigration Research

Based on the provided bar chart, India consistently holds the highest position in terms of immigration origin over a ten-year span, with a significant increase in immigration by 35.14% during this period.

In contrast, the other countries exhibited relatively smaller growth rates, indicating that India's immigration patterns have surpassed other countries in Asia.

Immigration Barchart

Chart Information

  • The barchart presents the top 10 countries in Asia in which immigration originates. Each bar in the chart corresponds to a specific country, providing a visual representation of the immigration patterns across various nations.
  • Based on the data provided by the United Nations for years 2010, 2015, and 2020.
  • The chart features switchable years through 3 buttons where the chart bars redraw themselves to match the country name.
  • Mouseover effects of the bars which display the exact immigration numbers depending on the country.

Data Research

The data used contains both quantitative and qualitative data. The immigration data is classified as quantitative continuous data as it indicates the quantity of immigrants moving from a country. The countries' data represents qualitative nominal data as it represents the different countries in Asia.
