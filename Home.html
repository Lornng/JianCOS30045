Immigration Issues and Research (2020)

Why 2020 was the selected year of investigation? First, because most of the big data was collected in a regular pattern of 5 years or a decade as it takes time for data mining, cleaning and validatiing. Although every country will record it's population and migration annually. However, it is not easy to collect a world data that include a significant amount of accurate and valuable data. From other prespective, the year 2020 was severely effected by the world pandemic. This would be intresting to investigate how it affects the migration as well. Therefore, the year 2020 was selected.
